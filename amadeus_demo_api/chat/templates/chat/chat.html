Figma vs Adobe XD / Figma or Adobe XD

Figma and Adobe XD are both design tools used for UX and UI design. Figma offers real-time collaboration and is browser-based, while Adobe XD is focused on creating wireframes and prototypes and is part of the Adobe Creative Cloud suite of tools.

Figma vs Sketch / Figma or Sketch

Both Figma and Sketch are popular design tools used by UX and UI designers. Figma offers more collaboration features and is browser-based, while Sketch is focused on vector design and is only available on Mac. The choice between the two ultimately comes down to personal preference and project requirements.
